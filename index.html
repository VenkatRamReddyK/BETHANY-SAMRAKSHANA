BETHANY SAMRAKSHANA

Bethany Samrakshana started in 2014 is a Charitable society which evisages a better and just society where in the least and the lost may find a home - a home in the real sense of the word.

Founder

Bethany Samrakshana is the second brain child of Rev.Fr.Joseph Vattaparambil a catholic priest,his first venture being the Bethany Karunalaya a full-fledged children's home and an old age home with 90 and 25 inmates respectively. Now he shifted his base to Dhonthi Village where the present Ashram is located .He has drawn his inspiration from the late Rev.Mar Ivanios O.I.C the founder of Malankara Catholic Church and the Bethany Congregation. He has an unquenching passion to reach out to the poor and needy, he never denies anyone who comes to him for shelter or otherwise.he is extremely dedicated to the cause.

Love And Care are our watchwords

Aim: Our aim is to bring about a transformation in the lives of the poor and the downtrodden .Keeping it in mind we started a home [Mother's Nest] for the orphans/semi-orphans; children picked up from streets and also children who are otherwise deprived of an opportunity to education due to parental negligence. At present our temporary Shelter houses around 60 children out of which 28 are school going [Boys&Girls], another 25 are children with special needs and few are going for vocational training. These days there is a growing awareness among the youngsters regarding giving back to the society , but they do not know what to do and how to do. Here we are looking forward to your goodwill and support to carry-on our mission of bringing about a smile on the faces of the less privileged. Be our partners in creating a better society by providing better for the rural and a home for the homeless. You can be assured every penny of yours will be put to the best use. You are most welcome to Bethany Samrakshana Home any time .
